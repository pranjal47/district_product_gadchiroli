Gadchiroli Product Achievement

  • Largest organic turmeric producer in Maharashtra (2023)
  • 1,20,000+ farmers under soil health mission
  • Record paddy yield: 3200 kg/hectare (2024)
  • 38% increase in millet cultivation since 2022
  • Best district for forest farming (minor produce)

Message from the District Collector

This exhibition platform highlights Gadchiroli’s products and achievements. It is a non-profit initiative created to inform consumers and support local producers.

District Collector Office, Gadchiroli

🌾 The District Collector Office, Gadchiroli “We are working under the guidance of the District Collector, Gadchiroli, to provide an exhibition platform that highlights the diverse products of our district. This initiative is designed to support local producers by offering a space for marketing and consumer awareness. It is not intended for profit or revenue generation, but rather to inform and connect consumers with the rich industrial and agricultural heritage of Gadchiroli. Through this platform, we aim to strengthen community identity, encourage sustainable practices, and empower tribal and rural entrepreneurs.” and manage the 'Gadchiroli Agri-Brand'.
